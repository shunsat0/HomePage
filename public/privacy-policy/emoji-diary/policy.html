Emoji Diary Privacy Policy

1. Collection of Personal Information

The App does not collect users' personal information.

2. Purpose of Information Use

The App uses the emoji diary data entered by users solely for providing the App's functionality.

3. Information Management

The diary data entered by users is stored only on the user's device and is not transmitted externally.

4. Provision to Third Parties

The App does not provide user information to third parties.

5. Security

Appropriate security measures are taken to protect user data. However, please note that the transmission of information via the internet is not completely secure.

6. Contact

For inquiries regarding this privacy policy, please contact us through the inquiry form within the App.

7. Changes to the Policy

This privacy policy may be changed as necessary. The revised policy will take effect from the time it is announced within the App.
